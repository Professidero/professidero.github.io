    The water in the well is evaporating, bring some water from the river to fulfill again !


    • Collect the glowing user manual to know about your objective
    • There is some gourds located around your camp fire. They are glowing when you're close enough to it to inform you that you can grab it (you can only have one gourd at the same time)
    • You can go to the river to fill your gourd
    • Fulfill the well with your gourd before it goes empty to win the game

    Context : University small project. The level's graphics assets were provided by our teacher, we had 15 hours to find a gameplay and implement it in the Unreal Engine

    Type : Action

    Platform : PC, tested on Windows

    When was it made : 2017

    Team : 3

    Technology : Unreal Engine

    What did I do : All the programming part was made by me while the missing assets (well model, UI elements, user manual and some enemy models which were not implemented) have been done by my teammates
