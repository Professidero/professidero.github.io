    Get in your spaceship and protect the Earth against the asteroid rains !


    • 2 players have to defend the earth by cooperating
    • When the players are close enough, they can activate a barrier to destroy everything which would try to pass through it or a player cans sacrifice himself to destroy an asteroid
    • Some collectible objects might appear from time to time, if 5 of them are collected, players get an enhanced barrier
    • Sometimes, a black hole might appear too, disturbing player's movements when they are close enough

    Context : University game jam project. A special game jam organized by the university mixing students ranging from second year of bachelor to last year of master degree. 4 school days to create a cool game which is based on the jam subject. The first day was spent working on a game design convincing enough for our teachers then we developed this game in 3 days

    Type : Co-op, action

    Platform : PC, tested on Windows

    When was it made : 2018

    Team : 8

    Technology : Unity/C#

    What did I do : All the programming part was made by me while the graphic assets, particle effects, game design and level design were made by my teammates. So I programmed the player's actions, barrier system, asteroids' (trivial) AI, black hole mechanic, collectible items Finally, I made tools to help my mates to create customized enemy waves, black hole and collectible items spawning
